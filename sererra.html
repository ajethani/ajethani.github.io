SERERRA

This is my story leading the design team for Sererra RentalSeries WebApplication. Updating the experience and bringing a fresh new look to Sererra RentalSeries.

My Role

As the lead designer, I worked directly under the founder and CEO of the company as well as being the main communicator between the product manager and the engineering team from start to finish.

My involvement in redesigning the RentalSeries caused the company to scaled significantly which not only lead in an increase of revenues and sales, but also getting attentions from companies that was unreachable before the redesign of the product (this was huge).

THE CHALLENGE

Massive Features, Minimal Design

The biggest challenge I faced was showing all the necessary information without overcrowding the page. Although it was easy to get carried away with showing a lot of information in one page, this was not a good idea. How do I combine a massive feature into a minimalist design?

THE APPROACH

Flexibility

As the lead designer, I started with researching the most used and most important features for the product. This way, I can hide some features yet giving users the control to these features whenever and wherever they need it the most.
